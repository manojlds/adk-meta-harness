# Deep Research Skill

Use this skill when you need to conduct comprehensive research that goes beyond
a simple lookup.

## When to Use

- Multi-faceted questions requiring synthesis across sources
- Topics with conflicting or evolving information
- Research requiring verification and cross-referencing
- Structured analysis of complex domains

## Research Process

1. Decompose the question into 3-5 focused sub-questions
2. For each sub-question, identify the most authoritative sources
3. Search using specific, targeted queries — not broad terms
4. Read source content fully before drawing conclusions
5. Cross-reference key claims across at least 2 independent sources
6. Synthesize into a structured response with clear citations

## Quality Checks

- Did I verify key claims with multiple sources?
- Did I distinguish fact from opinion or hypothesis?
- Did I flag areas of uncertainty or active debate?
- Are all citations traceable to specific sources?

See `references/research_methodology.md` for detailed methodology.
